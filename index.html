We can also extract gravitational wave data from our simulations. Single neutron stars produce comparatively weak gravitational waves since they evolve over longer periods of time and are usually less catastrophic than other sources. 

At the moment, LIGO considers neutron stars in our galaxy as the most important search targets for continuous gravitational waves. Our simulations can hopefully give us a realistic idea of what signals to expect in future searches.
